Optional lengthPenalty

lengthPenalty?: number

Exponential penalty to the length that is used with beam-based generation. It is applied as an exponent to the sequence length, which in turn is used to divide the score of the sequence. Since the score is the log likelihood of the sequence (i.e. negative), lengthPenalty > 0.0 promotes longer sequences, while lengthPenalty < 0.0 encourages shorter sequences.
